My First Week at AkiraChix

It has been two weeks since we moved into AkiraChix Campus. I would like to highlight my experience during the first week of my stay. I would term the whole week as Orientation Week, however the orientation was done in steps so that we would not be overwhelmed by the whole experience. I found the approach quite perfect as it allowed us to digest the information in bits and it made adjusting to the new environment easier since some of us were from different countries

Orientation

Orientation Meeting with Parents

On 1st February when we came, the institution held a meeting with our parents before they signed the contract. We were also present at the meeting. The meeting was held so that the parents would be told the details of the program. What the program entailed, what role they played, what role ,we, the students played what role the instituion played. They were informed about the benefits that will accrue from us taking the program and what requirements we will have to meet to be able to graduate. They were called upon to check up on us from time to time to see how we are doing and to also encourage us when things get tough. They were informed that we would not be allowed to leave the campus until after graduation. Our parents got to understand the consequences of exiting the program and the conditions that would lead to us being expelled by the institution. Afterwards they signed a contract with the institutionand said their goodbyes.

Orientation Meeting with Students

After our parents left, we later had a meeting that included the students and AkiraChix staff.The staff at akirachix was introduced to us and their roles in the institution was made known.We had follow-up meetings as the week progressed where they gave us bits and bits of information.

The information included:

  1. AkiraChix schedule:from the time we wake up to the time we go to school and all the activities in between
  2. Cleaning duties:The areas we would be cleaning, when and how we would be cleaning them.
  3. Meal Plans:What time we would be eating and the procedure wewould follow
  4. Classroom schedule:Timetable

Preparation for the classes

The trainors taught us how to use applications that we would be using in all our classes. We were taught how to write emails, how tou use slack, how to use google slides,google docs, google sheets and other google applications. We were also taught how to set up github and how to use the terminal.
